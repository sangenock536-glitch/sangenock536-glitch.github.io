Our Story

We Built the Site We Wished Existed

FreeStudyHub was started by students who were tired of sorting through sponsored lists, paywalled recommendations, and tools that turned out not to be free at all.

Our Mission

Making Great Study Tools Accessible to Every Student

Every student deserves access to the tools that help them learn — regardless of budget. The problem is that most "best tools" lists are written by marketers, not students. They recommend whatever pays the highest commission, and they bury the free alternatives.

FreeStudyHub was built to fix that. We test every tool we recommend. We write about what actually works. And we're transparent about how we keep the lights on (short version: affiliate links and ads — see our disclosure page).

What We Stand For

Our Values

🔬

Tested, Not Assumed

We use the tools we write about. Every review is based on real experience, not marketing copy.

💸

Genuinely Free

🚫

Honest Opinions

We say when tools have problems. If a free plan is too limited to be useful, we say so clearly.

🔍

Everything on this site is written with one audience in mind: students aged 15–24 trying to study smarter.

♻️

Regularly Updated

Study tools change their pricing and features often. We review our recommendations at least twice a year.

Our Process

How We Evaluate Tools

Every tool we feature goes through the same review process:

  1. Sign up for the free plan — we never pay to test a tool.
  2. Use it for at least two weeks in real study scenarios.
  3. Test it on mobile and desktop to check cross-device usability.
  4. Note every limitation of the free plan, not just the features.
  5. Write an honest review with clear pros, cons, and who it's best for.
  6. Re-review every 6 months as tools update their pricing and features.
