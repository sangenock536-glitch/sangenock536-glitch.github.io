Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how FreeStudyHub ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information about you when you visit our website at freestudyhub.com (the "Site").

By using this Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly — When you use our contact form, you may provide your name, email address, and message content.
  • Automatically collected data — We use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) that automatically collect information about how you use the Site. This may include your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the Site.
  • Cookies — We use cookies to understand Site traffic and improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, although some parts of the Site may not function correctly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your messages and enquiries
  • Understand how visitors use our Site so we can improve it
  • Monitor and prevent abuse or misuse of the Site
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics — for anonymous website traffic analysis. Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out via Google Ads Settings.
  • Affiliate partners — when you click affiliate links on our Site, the destination website may set its own cookies. We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use cookies for:

  • Analytics (understanding how visitors use our Site)
  • Advertising (displaying relevant ads via Google AdSense)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws, including:

  • The right to access personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@freestudyhub.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months, after which they are securely deleted. Analytics data is retained per Google's standard data retention policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is intended for students aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal data through our Site, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: hello@freestudyhub.com
  • Contact form: freestudyhub.com/contact
